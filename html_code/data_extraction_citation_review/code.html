Master Services Agreement

This Agreement is made as of January 15, 2024 (the "Effective Date") by and between LexGlobal Logistics Corp. and Enterprise Flow Inc.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Service Provider shall provide global supply chain management as defined in Exhibit A. The total aggregate liability under this agreement shall not exceed USD 5,000,000.00 for any single claim or series of related claims.

2. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement for convenience upon 90 days prior written notice to the other party. In the event of a material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately if the breach is not cured within 15 days.

3. GOVERNING LAW

This agreement and any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with it or its subject matter shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, USA.
